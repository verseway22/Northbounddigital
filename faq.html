Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you'd want to know before hiring me.

Straight answers. No jargon. If your question isn't here, just call — I'll answer it on the spot.

Pricing & Cost

How much does a website cost?

Starter sites start at $1,200. Business sites start at $2,200. Custom builds start at $3,500. The final price depends on the number of pages, features you need (like booking systems or galleries), and complexity. I'll give you an exact quote during our free consultation — no surprises after that.

Why is the $99/month required?

That covers everything it takes to keep a website running: hosting (where your site lives on the internet), your domain name, SSL security (the lock icon), daily backups, security monitoring, and up to 2 content updates per month. It also means you have a real person — me — on call if anything breaks. Think of it like a maintenance plan for your car, except it's less than your phone bill.

Can I cancel the monthly hosting?

Yes, anytime. No contracts, no cancellation fees, no tricks. If you cancel, I'll help you move your site to your own hosting so nothing breaks. You own everything — I'll hand over all the files.

Do I have to pay everything upfront?

No — it's 50% upfront, 50% on launch. You pay half to get started, I build your site and show you drafts, and you pay the other half once you're happy and we're ready to go live. You never pay the full amount before seeing real work.

Is a website really worth it for my small business?

Here's the math: if your new site brings in just 2 extra customers per week, and each customer spends $30–50, that's $3,000–5,000 in new revenue per year. A $1,200 site pays for itself in a few months. Beyond that, customers expect a website — 46% of people say they won't trust a business without one.
Process & Timeline

How long does it take to build my site?

Starter sites: 2–3 weeks. Business sites: 3–4 weeks. Custom builds: depends on scope. You'll see your first draft within the first week. The timeline mostly depends on how quickly you send me content (photos, menus, descriptions) and how many revision rounds we go through.

What do I need to provide?

At minimum: your business name, what you do, your hours, and how customers can reach you. Ideally, you'd also give me photos of your business, a menu or service list, and any branding you already have (logo, colors). Don't stress if you don't have all of this — I'll work with whatever you have and fill in the gaps.

What if I don't like the design?

You get multiple rounds of revisions with every tier (2 for Starter, 3 for Business, unlimited for Custom). I show you drafts along the way so we course-correct early. Nothing goes live until you look at it and say "that's exactly what I wanted." I'm not going to force a design on you.

Do I need to be technical?

Not at all. I handle all the technical stuff — domain setup, hosting, code, security, everything. You tell me what you want your site to do for your business, and I build it. When we need to communicate about the site, I speak plain English, not developer jargon.

Can I see drafts before it's done?

Yes — always. You'll get a private link to preview your site during the build. You can look at it on your phone, show it to your partner, share it with your team. I send progress updates regularly so there are zero surprises on launch day.
Ownership & Control

Do I own my website?

Yes — 100%. Your site, your domain, your code. If you ever want to take your site somewhere else, I hand over every file. No proprietary platforms, no lock-in, no "transfer fees." Some agencies hold your site hostage if you try to leave. I'll never do that.

Can I edit the site myself?

Your monthly plan includes 2 content updates per month — just text or email me what you need changed and I'll handle it, usually the same day. If you want to make edits yourself, I can set that up too. Most of my clients prefer to just text me — it's faster than logging into a dashboard.

What happens if I need more than 2 updates a month?

I'm flexible. Small stuff (swapping a photo, updating hours, fixing a typo) — I'll just do it, no charge. If you consistently need heavy edits like new pages or features, we'll work out a fair hourly rate. I'm not going to nickel-and-dime you over a text change.

What platform is my site built on?

Your site is custom-coded HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no WordPress, no Wix, no Squarespace. This means it loads faster, is more secure, and looks exactly the way we design it with no template limitations. The downside is you can't drag-and-drop edit it like Wix — but that's what I'm here for.
After Launch

What kind of support do I get?

You have my personal phone number. If your site goes down at 9pm on a Friday, you call me and I pick up. No support tickets, no chatbots, no "we'll get back to you in 3–5 business days." I also monitor your site for uptime and security issues proactively — you shouldn't have to worry about it.

Will my site show up on Google?

Yes. Every site I build includes SEO fundamentals — proper page titles, meta descriptions, fast load times, mobile optimization, and clean code that Google can read. For Business and Custom tiers, I also set up and optimize your Google Business Profile so you show up on Google Maps when someone searches for your type of business near them.

Can I add new features later?

Absolutely. Start with what you need now, add more when your business grows. Want to add online booking next quarter? A photo gallery for a new service? E-commerce? I can build onto your existing site without starting over. Most add-on features run $300–800 depending on complexity.

What if something breaks?

Call or text me. If it's a hosting issue, I fix it immediately — that's covered under your monthly plan. I also keep daily backups of your site, so even in a worst-case scenario, I can restore everything within hours. Your site has never "just disappeared" because I plan for problems before they happen.
Working With Me

Do you work with businesses outside the Bay Area?

Yes. I'm based in Newark, CA and specialize in Bay Area businesses because I'm local — but everything I do works over a phone call or video chat. I take clients anywhere in the US.

Why should I hire you instead of using Wix or Squarespace?

You could build a Wix site yourself — and it'll look like a Wix site. Templates are recognizable. They're slow. They limit what you can do. And when something goes wrong, you're on your own with a help article. With me, you get a site that's designed specifically for your business, loads fast, ranks better on Google, and comes with a real person who picks up the phone. The price difference is smaller than you think, and the quality difference is massive.

Why should I hire you instead of a big agency?

Agencies charge $5,000–15,000+ for the same work, and you'll talk to 3 different people before anyone touches your site. Then the junior designer who actually builds it has never been to your neighborhood. With me, you explain your business once, to one person, and that same person designs, builds, and supports your site. Faster. Cheaper. More personal.

I already have a website but it's bad. Can you fix it?

Depends on how bad. If it's a Wix or Squarespace site that just needs better content and design, sometimes a redesign within the same platform works. If it's truly outdated, a fresh custom build is usually better and not much more expensive than trying to patch something broken. Send me your URL and I'll tell you honestly which option makes more sense.
